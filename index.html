soundWaves

A more accessible Twitter experience with audio for shorts voice recordings and audio transcriptions.

I worked on this full stack application as part of the Northcoders final project on a team of 5 devs. It was carefully planned using wireframes and component trees. We used a Kanban Board (Trello) to keep track of progress and utilised standups every morning to communicate our previous work, plans, and blockers.
